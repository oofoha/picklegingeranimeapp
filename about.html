Meet The Team

The people behind your next favorite anime recommendation

Our Story

We created the Pickle Ginger App because we love anime.

We also love gathering with family and friends to enjoy watching anime together. For us, watching TV together with our parents, siblings, cousins, and friends are some of our favorite memories from childhood — actually, teenage-hood.

Things have changed since we were kids and accessing anime is easier than ever, but watching together isn't as easy. That's what inspired us to create Pickle Ginger: to help more people enjoy anime together.

The App

Your App For Personalized Anime Recommendations

Discover the ultimate companion for family movie nights with the Pickle Ginger Anime Adviser app. Designed for individuals and families alike, this app delivers personalized anime recommendations tailored to your viewing preferences.

With an intuitive interface, users can enter their preferences to quickly find anime — from action-packed adventures to heartwarming slice-of-life series.

Say goodbye to endless scrolling and let Pickle Ginger Anime Adviser guide you to your next favorite anime.

The Perks

The Pickle Ginger App includes:

  • A database with over 150+ anime series
  • Anime recommendations tailored for families
  • Updates based on community feedback
